# Example CV baseline – Jordan Ainsley (fictional)

This is a placeholder standing in for "the user's own CV" in the example dataset. It exists only so the example scores and briefing packs make sense together – it is not a real person, and nothing in it should be treated as a template to copy.

**Profile:** Product leader, ~9 years' experience, most recently Head of Product at a mid-size B2B SaaS company. Track record building 0-to-1 product lines and leading cross-functional teams of 6-12.

**Seniority level:** Head of Product / Director, occasionally VP-level roles at smaller companies.

**Right to work:** UK, no restrictions.

Compensation floor deliberately isn't listed here – it lives in `preferences-example.md` instead, since it's collected as an explicit, separate question (most real CVs don't state compensation at all) rather than read off the CV. See `schema/SCHEMA.md` → Preferences file.

When you replace the example data with your own, replace this file with your real CV (or point the skill at wherever your CV already lives) – see `README.md` → Getting started.
